About Warcraft 3: Reforged

Warcraft III: Reforged takes players back to the high-fantasy world of Azeroth, where they command armies and heroes from four distinct factions. These factions include the stalwart humans of the Alliance, the brutish orcs of the Horde, the mysterious Night Elves, and the undead forces of the Scourge.

Key Features

Remastered Graphics: Warcraft III: Reforged features completely reimagined graphics, including updated character models, animations, and environments. The remastered visuals bring the classic game into the modern era while retaining the iconic art style.

Rebalanced Gameplay: The gameplay has been rebalanced to ensure a more modern and competitive experience. While the core mechanics remain true to the original, certain adjustments have been made to improve overall balance and strategy.

Epic Campaigns: The game includes the original Warcraft III campaigns with enhanced storytelling and voiceovers. Players can experience the epic tales of Arthas Menethil, Thrall, Tyrande Whisperwind, and more as they navigate through a rich and immersive narrative.

World Editor: Warcraft III: Reforged comes with an updated World Editor, allowing players to create and share their custom maps and campaigns. This tool has been pivotal in the creation of numerous popular custom games, including the birth of the MOBA genre with Defense of the Ancients (DotA).

Multiplayer: The game supports modern multiplayer features, including matchmaking and custom game lobbies. Players can engage in competitive matches or team up with friends to conquer challenges.

Classic Soundtrack: The iconic music of Warcraft III has been re-recorded and remastered, providing a nostalgic and immersive audio experience.
